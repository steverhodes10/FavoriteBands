Green Day

Green Day is a punk rock band from Oakton, California. Green Day was very popular during the 1990s and early 2000s. Their 2004 album "American Idiot" sold 6.2 million albums.

The Black Keys

The Black Keys are a garage rock band from Akron, Ohio. They consist of two memebers: Dan Auerbach who is the lead singer and plays guitar, and Patrick Carney who plays drums.

Rage Against the Machine

Rage Against the Machine is a rap metal band from Los Angeles, California. They are known for their anti-establishment views. The band was together from 1991-2000, and 2007-2011.
